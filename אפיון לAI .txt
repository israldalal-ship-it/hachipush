WEBSITE SPECIFICATION
Project Name

החיפוש – רומן על מסע לחיפוש משמעות

Project Goal

Create a high-converting Hebrew landing page for a book called "החיפוש" written by Rabbi Adam Sinai.

The website should not feel like a religious website or a Kabbalah website.

The primary goal is to emotionally connect with visitors who are searching for meaning, identity, purpose, and truth in life.

The website should make visitors feel that the book understands their inner questions and struggles.

Secondary goal: collect leads through a free first chapter download.

Target Audience
Primary Audience

Young adults aged 18–35 who:

Search for meaning in life
Feel disconnected from modern society
Ask questions about identity and purpose
Feel there must be something deeper in life
Enjoy personal development, psychology, philosophy, spirituality

Important:

Do not present the book as a religious book.

Present it as a powerful story about self-discovery and meaning.

Secondary Audience

Adults aged 35–65 including:

Parents
Educators
Mentors
People interested in personal growth

They may purchase the book for themselves or as a gift.

Brand Positioning

The book is not:

A self-help book
A religious lecture
A Kabbalah textbook

The book is:

A compelling coming-of-age novel that introduces deep insights about the human soul through story.

Core Message

"There comes a moment when you realize life must be more than this."

The book follows a 17-year-old boy who begins questioning everything around him.

His journey becomes a search for truth, meaning, identity and purpose.

Modern

Page Structure
Section 1 — Hero

Headline:

יש רגע שבו אתה מרגיש שהחיים חייבים להיות יותר מזה

Subheadline:

יותר מבית ספר.
יותר מקריירה.
יותר מלעבור עוד יום ועוד יום בלי לדעת לאן כל זה הולך.

CTA:

הזמינו עכשיו

Secondary CTA:

קראו פרק ראשון במתנה

Hero image:

Book cover + emotional cinematic background.

Section 2 — What Is The Search

Headline:

זהו לא רק סיפור על נער אחד

Body:

Explain that the story follows a 17-year-old boy searching for truth, meaning and identity.

Emphasize that the questions he asks are questions every person eventually asks.

Section 3 — Questions Section

Display large emotional questions:

מי אני באמת?

למה לפעמים הכול מרגיש ריק גם כשהכול בסדר?

מה אני אמור לעשות עם החיים שלי?

איך מוצאים אמת בעולם מלא רעש?

Section 4 — Book Benefits

Three cards:

אמת

Section 5 — Book Excerpts

Display selected excerpts from the book.

Beautiful typography.

Allow multiple expandable excerpts.

Section 6 — Testimonials

Reader testimonials.

Separate:

Young readers
Parents
Adults

Display with photos if available.

Section 7 — About The Author

Headline:

מאחורי הספר עומד מסע חיים אמיתי

Summary:

Rabbi Adam Sinai spent decades researching meaning, spirituality, human consciousness and Kabbalah.

The book was inspired by both his own journey and his observations of young people searching for truth.

Keep this section brief.

The author supports the book.

The book remains the hero.
